Game Releases

Dungeon Chef

Release Date : 01 Apr 2017

Platform : Mobile

Game Engine : Unity

 

Take your cooking adventure to the next level by becoming a master chef in this RPG-style offline cooking simulation game. Fight and cook the monsters and climb your way to become a legendary chef in the world of Dungeon Chef!

Fantasista

Release Date : 08 Jun 2016

Platform : Mobile

Game Engine : Unity

 

In Fantasista you will feel the social competitive spirit at online simulation league. Join a team based on your favorite city with other players to build the best and most united team to conquer the world’s top football league and cup.

Juragan Hantu

Release Date : 30 Oct 2017

Platform : Mobile

Game Engine : Unity

 

Indonesia is known as an archipelago with abundant of resources. From its landscape and natural resources, to the treasures of the unseen. Reveal and capture Indonesia’s various ghosts and become the ultimate spiritual expert across the land in Juragan Hantu!

Juragan Terminal

Release Date : 16 Aug 2016

Platform : Mobile

Game Engine : Unity

 

Build your own public transportation terminal and become a Juragan Terminal (eng: Terminal Skipper)! Juragan Terminal is Agate’s collaboration piece with Own Games, the developer of the famous game Tahu Bulat.

Kuis Iseng Kaesang

Release Date : 07 Jan 2017

Platform : Mobile

Game Engine : Unity

 

Not only people recognize Kaesang Pangarep as the Indonesian President’s son, he is also well-known as a famous vlogger. It is known that he has a very unique way of interaction with his viewers. Expanding his throne of fame, Kaesang collaborates with Agate through the title of Kuis Iseng Kaesang.

Love Spice: Bumbu Cinta

Release Date : 17 Mar 2017

Platform : Mobile

Game Engine : Unity

 

Love Spice is a visual novel game that is initially designed for female mobile users. Each of the characters has its own unique animations based on their personality. With 13 multiple endings, Love Spice lets you choose your own fate.

Sahabat Anak Sholeh

Release Date : 17 Nov 2017

Platform : Mobile

Game Engine : Unity

 

Sahabat Anak Sholeh (SAS) is designed to provide early Islamic education for children with a fun and exciting way. Not only educational, SAS is also safe for children as it’s free of any ads and negative contents than the children may have inadvertently seen.

Valthirian Arc 2

Release Date : 02 Dec 2013

Platform : Desktop, Windows

Game Engine : Adobe Flash

 

Valthirian Arc 2 is the sequel of the previous series. Unlike the prequel, Valthirian Arc 2 begins by the quest for a new principal for one of its brand new academy in Valthiria. Similar with other school in Valthiria, this school is also responsible to nurture world’s finest soldiers, mages, and scouts.

Reon Puzzle Fighter

Release Date : 05 Dec 2016

Platform : Mobile

Game Engine : Unity

 

Especially for the Reonites, Agate collaborating with re:ON Comics in developing this fun Reon Puzzle Fighter, an official puzzle match 3 game. Similar with Agate as Indonesia’s leading game company, re:On Comics is Indonesia’s renowned comic publisher which aims to develop the local works.
